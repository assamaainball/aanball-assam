Welcome to Aainball Association, Assam

Aainball is a new-age sport fostering discipline, strength, and unity. Our association works toward spreading the game across Assam's districts, empowering youth, and creating future champions.

About Aainball

Aainball is a physically engaging, fast-paced sport inspired by ancient Indian sports ethics. The game builds stamina, coordination, and sportsmanship. The Assam association is leading awareness, tournaments, and coach development across the state.

District Association

We have established district-level bodies across 25+ districts of Assam. These associations conduct training, register players, organize events, and report regularly to the state unit.

Franchise

The Aainball League introduces a franchise-based system to grow competition and funding. Districts can form teams under franchise leadership and compete in state-level tournaments annually.
